InsightPilot is a data analysis platform designed to make data analysis accessible and straightforward for users of all technical skill levels. It provides tools for data uploading, preprocessing, visualization, and sharing, all through an intuitive user interface.

You can upload data by navigating to the 'Upload Dataset' section of the tool. InsightPilot supports various file formats like CSV, XLSX, and JSON. Simply select the file from your device, and the system will handle the rest.

InsightPilot offers a range of preprocessing options including cleaning, normalizing, standardizing data, handling missing values, outlier detection, and more to prepare your data for analysis.

Yes, you can share your preprocessed datasets with other users via email. Just enter the recipient's email address, and the system will send them a link to access the dataset.

InsightPilot allows you to create various types of visualizations such as bar charts, line graphs, pie charts, heat maps, scatter plots, and more to help you better understand your data.

The chatbot in InsightPilot can assist you with any data-related queries. Simply initiate a chat through the chat interface, and the chatbot will provide you with the information or guidance you need.
